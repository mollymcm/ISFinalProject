the name game

I am a lot of things. According to Old English, I am either bitter or a star of the sea. American slang says I am a party drug. Science says I am a species of fish.

Of all of these, my favorite is bitter.

While Urban Dictionary defines bitter as "to be perpetually pissed off", that's just one interpretation. Think about all those amazing bitter foods: dark chocolate, kale, peppermint, and citrus fruits. When we eat these foods, there is stimulation! There is sensation! There is feeling!

If being bitter means that I am someone who brings sensation, I will gladly go by that meaning of my name.
